Brace yourself for a rollercoaster of emotions as noble houses clash in epic battles for power and the coveted Iron Throne. Top Castles of Westeros
Red Keep

The Red Keep, also known as the Red Castle, is the central fortress in King's Landing, capital of the Seven Kingdoms. It's a symbol of power, home to the ruling monarch, and the site of political intrigue, housing the Iron Throne.

Main Houses
Stark House

House Stark, based in Winterfell, is one of the Great Houses of Westeros. Known for their honor, they rule the North and are symbolized by a direwolf. Their motto, 'Winter is Coming,' reflects their resilience in the face of adversity and their deep connection to the northern lands.

Main Dragons
Drogon

Drogon is a magnificent and fearsome dragon, one of three born to Daenerys Targaryen. With jet-black scales and enormous wings, Drogon is the largest and most formidable of the trio. He is named after Daenerys's late husband, Khal Drogo. As her trusted companion, Drogon shares a unique bond with Daenerys and plays a pivotal role in her journey to reclaim the Iron Throne. His fiery breath and power make him a symbol of her strength and authority.

Main Armies
Daenerys Targaryen's army

Daenerys Targaryen's army, known as the Unsullied, is a formidable force in the Game of Thrones world. Comprised of highly trained slave soldiers, they are renowned for their unwavering loyalty, discipline, and fearsome combat skills. Led by the Mother of Dragons, Daenerys, they play a crucial role in her quest for the Iron Throne, embodying her vision of a just and free realm.
